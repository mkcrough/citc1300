About Michael's Mountain Bike Shop

Here at Michael's Mountian Bike shop we have been in business for over 25 years. We are capable of outfitting the beginner up to a professional team. We have it all. We have bikes to rent for the day, week or month. More if you need it. We specialize in fitting your needs.

We offer bikes for everyone. Have kids? We offer a huge selection of kids bikes. We have 26's, 29's and fat tires. We also offer electric bikes for those that don't fancy pedaling. We have bike shorts, shirt, helmets and a boat load of accessories to add to your collection. Stop by and let us help you get started.
