The Masters Tournament

About The Masters Tournament

Also known as The Masters or The U.S. Masters is one of the fourth major tournaments of the Grand Slam. It takes place in the first week of April and it inaugurates the key games. It is always held in the same place, Augusta National Golf Club, a private course in Georgia. There are no qualifying tournaments, to take part in The U.S. you have to receive an invitation. The winner of the game receives a green jacket which is a symbol of the club membership. The jacket has to be returned the next year. From this moment, the winner can wear it only the club’s area e.g. during the annual Champions Dinner. The first The U.S. Masters was won by Horton Smith. His award amounted to 1500$, which from today’s perspective seems incredible. The most awarded player in the history of The U.S. Masters was Jack Nickalus.

  • Where: Augusta, GA
  • Course: Augusta National Golf Course
  • Date: April 7th -April 10th
  • Winner: Scottie Schaffler
